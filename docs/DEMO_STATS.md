## Systemic Failures Our AI Addresses

### Why African Patients Are Misdiagnosed

| Root Cause | Percentage of Cases |
|------------|---------------------|
| Anchoring bias (assumed most common local disease) | **80%** (12/15 benchmark cases) |
| Required diagnostic test not locally available | **67%** (10/15 cases) |
| Disease considered "not present" in that region | **53%** (8/15 cases) |
| Symptom overlap with common diseases | **100%** (all 15 cases share ≥3 symptoms with wrong dx) |

## What MedSyn Investigator Does

| Capability | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| Synthesizes symptoms + labs + geography simultaneously | Catches what specialists miss in isolation |
| Cross-references Orphanet, PubMed, WHO regional data | No internet required per clinician |
| Applies geographic epidemiological context | Altitude, soil type, endemic zones as diagnostic discriminators |
| Flags "treatment non-response" as a red flag | Turns a failure into a clue |
| Recognizes multi-system non-classic clusters | What single-specialty clinicians structurally cannot see |
| Available at point-of-care in rural Africa | No specialist referral needed to get a differential |

## Competition Context

MedSyn Investigator is purpose-built for SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being):

- **Target 3.4**: Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases
- **Target 3.8**: Achieve universal health coverage including access to quality essential health-care services
- **Target 3.b**: Support research and development of medicines for diseases affecting developing countries

> Africa bears **24% of the global disease burden** with only **3% of global health workers** and **< 1% of global health expenditure**. MedSyn Investigator is clinical intelligence infrastructure for the continent.
